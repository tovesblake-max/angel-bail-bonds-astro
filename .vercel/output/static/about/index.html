Everything You Need to Know About Angel Bail Bonds

What is Angel Bail Bonds?

Angel Bail Bonds is a professional bail bond company based in Stillwater, Oklahoma. Founded and operated by Karri McBride, Angel Bail Bonds has been serving Central Oklahoma for over 15 years. The company provides 24/7 emergency bail bond services for all types of criminal charges across five Oklahoma counties.

Who founded Angel Bail Bonds?

Angel Bail Bonds was founded by Karri McBride, who has over 15 years of experience in the bail bond industry. Karri is a licensed bail bondsman in the state of Oklahoma and operates Angel Bail Bonds as a women-owned business.

Where is Angel Bail Bonds located?

Angel Bail Bonds is located in Stillwater, Oklahoma, and serves five Central Oklahoma counties: Payne County (including Stillwater and surrounding communities), Pawnee County, Lincoln County (including Chandler, Prague, Stroud, and Meeker), Kay County (including Ponca City, Blackwell, Newkirk, and Tonkawa), and Noble County (including Perry, Billings, Morrison, and Red Rock).

What services does Angel Bail Bonds offer?

Angel Bail Bonds handles all types of bail bonds including felony bail bonds, misdemeanor bail bonds, DUI/DWI bail bonds, domestic violence bail bonds, drug charge bail bonds, outstanding warrant bail bonds, surety bonds, and juvenile bail bonds. They offer flexible payment plans and accept most forms of payment.

Is Angel Bail Bonds available 24/7?

Yes, Angel Bail Bonds provides 24-hour bail bond services, 7 days a week, 365 days a year, including all holidays. Arrests can happen at any time, and Angel Bail Bonds is always ready to help. Call (405) 614-3000 at any hour for immediate assistance.

What is Angel Bail Bonds' reputation?

Angel Bail Bonds has a perfect 5.0-star rating on Google with over 27 verified reviews. Clients consistently praise Karri McBride for her compassion, professionalism, responsiveness, and ability to make the bail process less stressful during difficult times.

How much does a bail bond cost with Angel Bail Bonds?

In Oklahoma, the standard bail bond premium is 10% of the total bail amount set by the court. Angel Bail Bonds offers flexible payment plans to help families afford bail during financially challenging times. The exact cost depends on the bail amount set by the judge for each specific case.

Does Angel Bail Bonds serve Spanish-speaking clients?

Yes, Angel Bail Bonds offers services in Spanish for the Hispanic community in Central Oklahoma. Spanish-speaking families can call (405) 614-3000 for assistance in Spanish at any time.

What makes Angel Bail Bonds different from other bail bond companies?

Angel Bail Bonds stands out for several reasons: over 15 years of local experience, a perfect 5.0-star Google rating, women-owned business, 24/7 availability including holidays, coverage across five Oklahoma counties, flexible payment plans, bilingual services (English and Spanish), and deep knowledge of the Payne County court system and local jails.
